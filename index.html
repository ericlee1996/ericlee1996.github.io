Mission

111(Triple-one): 1 company, 1 market, 1 world

111 makes it simpler for unaccredited consumers to gain trust in hardware startups by providing services in workflow verification, market due-diligence, and pre-order purchases.
We are looking to gain investment and partnerships to build out platforms based on our framework.



Workflow Verification

Using smart contract infrastructure as our middleware, our software creates smart contract instances for each task completed, checks them against periods between substantial milestones, and marks each task as verified or not verified.

Market Due-Diligence

Before Know Your Customer and Anti-Money Laundering methods are employed, our system weighs market participants against one another in a public forum to enable market specific peer review.

Pre-Order Purchases

According to an independent study on Kickstarter in 2015, 35% of Kickstarter project backers did not receive products on time. Accurately tracking teams and their progress by providng more thorough information through the 111 token will increase confidence, set realistic expectations, and prevent delays.

Test Case: Hard Tech Fund

111 was employed by UC Davis' Hard Tech Fund to better track their teams, manage interest from investors and corporate partners, and provide alternate sources of fundraising.

  • Workflow Verification

    To best verify workflows in this brand new program, we made sure all Hard Tech Fund teams used Toggl to track the hours put into their project, Github to share any programming projects, and Google Drive for managing meeting notes, presentations, drawings, and budgets. For each activity completed on each platform, a new task token is generated in stored on the blockchain as proof of work being done by a team.

  • Market Due-Diligence

    The main event in which both teams and interested investment groups and corporate partners would interact was the Hard Tech Fund Demo Day. All interested parties, both ones that could attend the event and those who could not, were made to fill out this form:

    Link to the forms

    From this form, points were awarded to interested parties for filling out each field. These points could then be used to make a pre-order on a particular team.

  • Pre-Order Purchases

    For the purposes of the Hard Tech Fund Demo Day, the 111 tokens were simulated and are to be released on 9/19, with the launch of real 111 pre-order tokens still under discussion.
